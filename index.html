Our Goal

We set an ambitious goal for ourselves

Help Stop The War In Ukraine!

How: by uniting Ukrainians with the art fans around the world who are willing to support Ukraine in such a tough period.

Thus, we are aiming to raise and pass over to military and volunteers at least $1,000,000 in crypto currency, by selling NFT arts of Ukrainian designers and artists.

Collected money will be transferred to official Ukrainian Volunteer accounts as well as distributed to local Volunteering centers needs (all donations will be tracked and shared).

Collect arts we MAKE while sirens are out loud.

Let’s fight for PEACE in any possible ways.

Let’s stop this war TOGETHER and save us from aggression
